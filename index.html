Usability Guide

Interactions

ActionHow
Drag handleClick and drag either circle to move the start or end date independently.
Drag rangeClick and drag the red bar between handles to slide the entire range while keeping its duration fixed.
Click dateClick the blue-underlined dates above the slider to open a calendar picker for precise date entry.
Preset buttonsClick a preset (7d, 30d, 90d, etc.) to jump to a common range. Active preset is highlighted.
Nudge arrowsClick the left/right arrows to shift the entire range earlier or later. Shifts by the range duration or 7 days, whichever is smaller.
Zoom in/outZoom into the current selection for finer control, or zoom out to see more range. Reset returns to full 3-year view. Labels adapt to zoom level.
MAX indicatorAppears automatically when the full 3-year range is selected. Disappears when the range is reduced.

Configuration Options

VariableDescription
threeYearsAgoControls how far back the slider goes. Change today.getFullYear() - 3 to any number of years.
defaultStartThe initial left handle position. Set to any Date timestamp within the range.
defaultEndThe initial right handle position. Defaults to today.
stepSnap increment. Currently 24 * 60 * 60 * 1000 (1 day). Use 7 * ... for weekly steps.
behaviourSet to 'drag' to enable dragging the connected range bar. Other options: 'tap', 'snap'.

Accessibility Notes

  • Monospace font prevents layout shift when dates change between single and double digit days.
  • Blue underline on dates signals clickability (follows link affordance conventions).
  • Grab cursor on handles and range bar communicates draggability.
  • Day count badge provides at-a-glance feedback on the selected duration.
